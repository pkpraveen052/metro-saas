Captivating templates

Using the alluring templates on offer with Odoo Custom Reports app customize your reports( Contemporary, Clean, etc)

Reports

Customize 7 types of reports- Invoice, Sales Order, Payment Receipts, RFQs, Delivery Notes, and Picking Slips.

Provision To Add Extra Content

Extra contents can be displayed with the reports such as Brochures, Terms & Conditions, Advertisements, etc via pdfs and Upload Custom HTML or both while they are printed.

Upload Custom HTML

Amount In Words

This feature automatically converts an amount in numerical figure to words. The conversion can also be done in various languages such as English, French, German, Russian, and much more. It can be turned on/off as per requirement.

Modify Colors & Adjust Boldness:

You can alter the colors of heading text, company name, total value, table contents, payment history texts, etc from the color picker or bold them effortlessly.

Display Tagline & Logo

Display the best business logo to highlight your theme or use a smart tagline that makes the heads turn!
Header Configuration
Edit Tag Line

Pick Font Sizes

The size of the font sets the readability of the content. Report template allows you to individually arrange the font size of the header content, body, and footer of reports. Apart from that, background, display amount and table colors can also be set.

Footer Configurations

Using the footer configurations, footnotes can be enabled or disabled, the text color & size can be changed
Custom Footnote

Watermarks

Watermarks can be enabled/disabled on the reports. The custom watermarks settings allow you to set custom watermarks(if turned on). The watermark can be your company name, logo or a different image. It can be scaled & rotated while its transparency & font size/color can be altered.

E-signatures & Custom Footers

Users can upload their e-signatures or simply draw them in the app. Using custom footers, more details can be added at the end of the report.

Preview

This feature allows you to preview your report once you are done with your customizations

Reset Style

If you require to set the report customizations to its default state, simply use the Reset Style option in the top left corner.

Report email (send copy to)/watermark

If the Report email (send copy to)/watermark in global settings has e-mail addresses & the individual setting doesn’t include any email addresses, then the email copies will be sent preferentially as per the global settings. In case the report/watermark setting has mentioned email addresses in their local settings, those email addresses will be preferred over global addresses.

Custom Layout Option

If you need to customize the reports with the app, you have to turn on the Custom layout option on. If you don’t turn it on, the reports will adhere to the default Odoo settings.

About Custom Report Layouts

If you are looking forward to draft an alluring report, you need to pay attention to the smallest aspects of it. And, Custom Report Layouts app can be your trusted ally in accomplishing that. Packed with multiple report templates, table & footer configurations, watermarks, multi-language functionalities, etc, it is an absolute delight for the admins to add a certain degree of uniqueness to the reports. The app scores high on simplicity & the easy-to-operate app can effortlessly manage report creation in Odoo.
